Dunmore Town

What is Dunmore Town?

Dunmore Town is a town in the Bahamas. It has a population of 1,762 (2010 census). It is the only town at Harbour Island, which is located just east from North Eleuthera.

Why do you want to go to Dunmore Town?

The town's vibrant culture as well as its pink beaches make Dunmore Town a must see on anyone's trip to the Bahamas.
