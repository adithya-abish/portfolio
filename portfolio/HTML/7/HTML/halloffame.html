Hall of Fame

Cristiano Ronaldo

Who Am I?

Cristiano Ronaldo played for Real Madrid from 2009 to 2018, becoming the club’s all-time leading scorer with 450 goals. Known for his unmatched goal-scoring ability and athleticism, he won four Champions League titles and two La Liga titles, leaving a massive legacy at the club.

Ronaldo Nazário

Who Am I?

Ronaldo Nazário, also known as "El Fenómeno," played for Real Madrid from 2002 to 2007. Despite injury issues, he was a fan favorite for his explosive pace and finishing, scoring 104 goals in 177 games and winning one La Liga title.

Zinedine Zidane

Who Am I?

Zinedine Zidane played for Real Madrid from 2001 to 2006 and later became one of their most successful managers. As a player, he dazzled with his elegance and vision, most famously scoring a stunning volley in the 2002 Champions League final. As manager, he led the team to three straight Champions League titles (2016–2018).
